LLMOps Report covers the production engineering of LLM systems — evaluation pipelines, online observability, cost and latency tradeoffs, prompt versioning, deployment architecture, and the operational discipline that keeps language models reliable at scale.

## What we cover

- **Evaluation pipeline design** — offline eval, online eval, and the tooling that makes evaluation trustworthy
- **Production observability** — instrumentation, latency profiling, and debugging degraded model behavior
- **Cost management** — API cost optimization, model selection tradeoffs, and self-hosted vs. API cost analysis
- **Prompt versioning** — managing prompt changes, rollback strategy, and experiment tracking
- **Deployment architecture** — serving infrastructure, scaling patterns, and failure modes at load

## Who reads this

ML engineers, platform engineers, and technical leads operating LLMs in production. The focus is on practical patterns — what breaks, how to instrument it, and how to ship improvements without breaking what works.
